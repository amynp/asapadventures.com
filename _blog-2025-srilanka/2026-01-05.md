It was an extremly early morning as we got up and hopped on the plane for our flight to Kuala Lumpur. We had decided to stop over here as it broke up our flight and I got a business class flight out of Kuala Lumpur. Whilst we were here we wanted to go to Uncle Rodgers resturant. The flight was uneventful and before we knew it we were in Malaysia.

The airport in Kuala Lumpur was super easy and organised, we got our bags quickly and then headed out to order a grab to the city. The last time we were in Kuala Lumpur was 13 years ago and man have things changed. It is much more fancy now and has crazy highways to get into the city. The hotel we were staying at was an apartment and was so fancy.

We finally made it to Uncle Rodgers resturant. Uncle Rodger is a youtuber who is known for making fun of people for making fried rice, in particular Jamie Oliver. He is like an overacting sterotype of Asians. We have wanted to go here since he opened it a year ago and a 1 night stopover provided the perfect oppurtunity to visit. It was so over the top themed,,, i loved it. The food was quiet good too. We had a quick look at a few shops before heading back to the hotel.
