We both hit the run down and slightly sick part of travel today which means it took a very very long time to get going. We finally got ourselves moving at lunch time and headed down to a little resturant near our hotel.

We were about to head back to the hotel to get our wet weather gear when a storm hit and the rain came bucketing down. We thought we would wait til it got a bit lighter. It took a while and then we had to walk back through the huge puddles that had formed.
