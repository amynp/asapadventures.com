Oh travel days! How we love them... We had to be up early for our drive to Brunei airport. They drop you off 3 hours before the flight which was way to early for how quick we got through that airport. We had a coffee, Anthony fixed up some of my blogs and  we went manget shopping and we still had hours before our flight. However the flight itself was great. I had booked the aisle and window seat and hoped no one booked the middle and it turns out no one booked the middle. We had a spare seat the whole flight.

Tonight we were going on a food tour with Lost Plate tours. We have previously done thier Cambodian tour and it was great so when I saw they did one in Bangkok I signed right up. We met up with Tukky who would be our guide for the night. As soon as I met her I knew we were in for a night! I got a huge hug and then she excitedly told us it was just us on the tour. She was so bubbly and full of energy. We jumped in our Tuk Tuk with Tukky sitting at my feet and leaning on my leg and off we went to explore Bangkok through food.

Stop 1: Tom Yum Wontons and noodles at Trok Rong Moo Noodle
<div class="center-image"><img src="/images/blogs/2025srilanka/23-12-2025/PXL_20251223_100131366.jpg_compressed.JPEG"/></div>

A chinese pork noodle store  that has blended Thai and 
chinese food. This dish blends flavorful tom yum ingredients like lemongrass, galangal, and kaffir lime with crispy fried wontons. Served alongside a bowl of tom yum noodles. It was spicy!!!! but so so good.

Stop 5: Dinner?
We went to Kaiyang Boran which is a resturant that has been around for ages we had minced pork with aromatic herbs (Laab), fresh papaya salad (Som Tum), fragrant sticky rice, and meatballs fried with lime leaves. It was so good and so many famous people have been here. But man we were getting so full.

Stop 6: Last stop dessert
Bangkok’s favorite sweet treat is what we were eating it is a combination of seasonal fruit and popular add-ons, all swimming in fresh coconut milk. It was pretty good.

<div class="center-image"><img src="/images/blogs/2025srilanka/23-12-2025/PXL_20251223_121720455.MP.jpg_compressed.JPEG"/></div>

I feel like we travelled all over Bangkok and we are just so full and walked out. The tour was amazing! Tukky was amazing too. She felt like she had adopted us by the end of the tour.
