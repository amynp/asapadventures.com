Hello,

We waste zero time around here travelling. A few months ago we saw a cheap deal to the South Pacific again. We enjoyed our last trip but were not the biggest fans of the carnival ship. We are big Royal Caribbean fans and when this deal was for one of our favourite ships Anthem of the Seas we decided to give the South Pacific another go on a cruise line that we know does things well. So after work we were off to the airport and on our way to Sydney. We got a upgrade to business class so it was actually the most relaxed flight in the world.

The crew on qantas were really great and we got a cheese platter for a meal. 

<div class="center-image"><img src="/images/blogs/2026southpacificcruise/02-04-2026/PXL_20260402_085246248.jpg"/></div>

When we arrived in Sydney our bags were the first out which has never happened to me in the whole time we have travelled. We grabbed an uber to the hotel. We settled in and then we went for a walk for food. Anthony saw quite a few people walking around with sticks with mear on them. Anthony decided we needed to find whereever they were from. We ended up finding the place around the corner from the hotel. We ordered a few sticks of meat and taking it back to the hotel before calling it a night.
