I wanted to visit Hyde Park Barracks as it is a UNESCO World hertitage site so we made our way over there. The Hyde Park Barracks is a former barracks, hospital, convict accommodation, mint and courthouse riginally constructed between 1817 and 1819 as a brick building and compound to house convict men and boys. It was free to look around and worth a drop in.
